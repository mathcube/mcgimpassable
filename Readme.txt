Impassable Terrain mod for Hearts of Iron 4

This mod is intended to add additional impassible terrain to parts of the world to improve gameplay.

#changelog
v1.1.0
	Resources in western and central australia have been changed to match the Increased Resources mod
v1.0.0
	-Initial Release
	-The Arctic coast of Siberia has been made impassable
	-Central Australia has been made impassable
	-The weastern coast of Australia has been moved from Central Australia to Western Australia
